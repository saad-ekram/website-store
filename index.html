about vision

vision™ is the world’s leading lifestyle brand for gamers.

the triple-headed snake trademark of vision is one of the most recognized logos in the global gaming and esports communities. with a fan base that spans every continent, the company has designed and built the world’s largest gamer-focused ecosystem of hardware, software and services.

vision’s award-winning hardware includes high-performance gaming peripherals and Blade gaming laptops. vision’s software platform, with over 175 million users, includes vision Synapse (an Internet of Things platform), vision Chroma rgb (a proprietary rgb lighting technology system supporting thousands of devices and hundreds of games/apps), and vision Cortex (a game optimizer and launcher). vision also offers payment services for gamers, youth, millennials and generation z. vision Gold is one of the world’s largest game payment services, and vision fintech provides fintech services in emerging markets. Founded in 2005, vision is dual headquartered in Irvine (california) and Singapore with regional headquarters in Hamburg and Shanghai. vision has 19 offices worldwide and is recognized as the leading brand for gamers in the usa, europe and china.
